# Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP)

CISSP course and exam preparation:
https://www.learnzapp.com/apps/cissp/ 

Ensure that you complete all the practical tests.

After completing each test, go through all questions and answers to further solidify your understanding for both correct and wrong questions.

The CISSP exam will be DIFFERENT from these practical tests, but it is important to go through them to understand the concepts or idea that the exam will ask. 

For any concept that needs further understanding when reviewing the practical tests, use chatGPT. it really helps in the explanation of security concepts.
